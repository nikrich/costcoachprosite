About

CCP assists businesses in effectively costing it’s products, applying different profit margins to generate accurate quotations for any job or project and assess the impact of an order on current production loads. CCP further provides the business with future raw material and labour requirements and the business owner with detailed Income & Cash flow statements to assist in the effective day-to-day management of the business. COST COACH PRO’s ease-of-use is particularly appropriate for small and medium-sized businesses, where financial skills are not always as well developed as the inherent technical skills. The CCP software is complemented by a training programme which provides the business’ relevant staff with both an general understanding of the financial principles of a business and in the specific use of the CCP software.To obtain more information on CCP, attend one of our regular information sessions or arrange for a personal demonstration, please feel free to contact us via e-mail at hein@costcoachpro.com

CCP Training & Support

The client receives initial training in both the principles of product costing and financial management and the use of the CCP software. Thereafter the client is assisted through telephonic and electronic support.

Product costing & financial management
  • General Principles of Gross Profit
  • Achieving Breakeven
  • Using all the different costs in the business in to Cost your Product
  • Making effective business decisions from knowing your Gross Profit
Using the ccp software
  • Inputting Customer, Product, Materials, Labour & Overhead data
  • Generating Quotes & using the Financial & Production information to obtain the right profit margin
  • Using the Accounting section to raise timeous Invoices and control interim & final cash receipts
  • Using the Materials & Labour requirements schedules to improve the daily management of your business
  • Using the Income & Cash flow statements to manage the general health of your business

A CCP Commitment to the Client on an industry basis

The Management of Cost Coach Pro is committed to assisting it’s clients in enhancing the effectiveness of their businesses by developing and expanding it’s product offering. To this end, it is continuously talking to it’s clients and to relevant industry bodies to assess specific areas of need.

Future software developments which have been scheduled as a result of these discussions are:

  • Integration of the Invoicing facility into the main accounting packages currently in use
  • Introduction of a pre-set production activity listing developed in collaboration with each specific industry
  • Quoting for off-site sales representatives through an integrated mobile application
  • Enhanced training through the provisioning of e-learning modules
